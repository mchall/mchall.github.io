ABOUT

Time your jumps from planet to planet, avoid the obstacles thrown in your path and blast off to safety with your rocket ship!

Play through 100 levels that will test your reaction skills!

Are you COSMIC enough to clear all the levels?!? Can you collect all the coins? Can you finish the levels while the counter is still warming up? Can you beat the harder COSMIC levels? Then, and only then, can you call yourself, truly, COSMIC!


FEATURES

- 100 levels to master! Get the best times and collect all the coins!

- 40 colorful characters to unlock!

- 9 rocketships to unlock! Blast off in style!

- Colorful voxel graphics and retro arcade sounds

- Optional retro TV game effect

- Full controller support
