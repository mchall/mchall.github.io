Description

Code a DumbBot to follow your instructions. Battle other DumbBots, or just mess around in the sandbox.

Compete in DeathMatch, Heist, Battle Royale or solo scenarios.

Learn the basics of programming, and improve your AI in the sandbox. The biggest brain wins this game.

Features

  • Easy to learn drag-and-drop scripting
  • Play solo missions, then play against other DumbBots and see who comes out victorious
  • Modes available: Battle Royale, Deathmatch, Heist and Survival
  • Customize your DumbBot squad
  • Vibrant, colorful maps
  • Create your own scenarios in Creative Mode
