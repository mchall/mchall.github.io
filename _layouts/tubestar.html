Description

TubeStar is a YouTube simulator where you manage channels to try and become a mega TubeStar!

Try become a viral sensation, earn enough from ad income to quit your job, or pollute the internet with videos so bad they have to be shared.

Features

  • Run your own simulated YouTube channel and feel like a rising internet star!
  • Increase your skills and earn more subscribers as you rise to the top!
  • Discover and watch real YouTube content! Maybe you'll find your new favourite channel!
  • Optional 'Creative Commons' mode for content creators.
  • Playable in English, Russian, Polish, Portuguese, Spanish, German, Swedish, Dutch, French,Indonesian, Thai, Chinese and Czech.
